The Clay General by Kim Xiong

Materials needed

  • Book
  • Mesh bag
  • Plastic Case
  • Mixing Bowl
  • Measuring cups & spoons
  • Cookie sheet
  • Clay tool
Click here to get started


The Clay General is happy to tell everyone who will listen about his courage and cleverness. But his boastful stories are put to the test in this lovely book that teaches an important life lesson while showcasing the art of Chinese ceramics.



Explore the book together

  • Was the General really brave? Was he smart?
  • After finishing the book, talk about what might happen after the Clay General melts in the puddle of water.
